Bible + SDABC

Privacy and Data Processing

Overview

Bible + SDABC does not collect, store, or sell personally identifiable information from users through the app. We do not request your name, email address, phone number, or financial information.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates third-party services that may process technical device information for advertising, analytics, or subscription management purposes:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising). Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat (Subscriptions and In-App Purchases). Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Firebase Remote Config (Remote configuration without collecting personal information). Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

These services may receive information such as device identifiers, approximate IP address, device model, operating system, country/language, and technical usage events. Any processing of this information is governed by the respective privacy policies of these providers.

Advertising and Tracking (iOS)

On iOS, we may request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework to improve the relevance of advertisements. You can change your decision at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. Denying this permission will not affect your ability to use the app.

In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed securely by the Apple App Store through RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment card information. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from your Apple ID settings: apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. The third-party services listed above retain information according to their respective privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or app functionality. The most current version will always be available on this page.
